Stephen Curry is a professional basketball player, often considered the best shooter of all time. 
He plays for the Golden State Warriors and is known for making three-point shots from very far away. 
Curry changed the way basketball is played by making long-distance shooting a big part of the game. 
He has won several championships with his team and is known for his great dribbling and passing skills.
Off the court, he's also involved in charity work and has his own business ventures.
